Agreement

Because of the GDPR you need to have some sort of permission from every person to store some of his/her personal information.

You could for example create an email-folder for storing any mails containing such permissions and store in the field agreement the according date to be able to find the permission fastly if needed.

If you have your permissions on physical paper, you could leave here a mark like red folder, page 24

For the exact handling please consider the regulations and requirements of your country and organization.
